Site Purpose

The purpose of my website is to act as a hub for events and activities in the southern Idaho region. Idaho's beautiful vistas and exhilarating adventures draw people from all over the intermountain west. With popular destinations such as Bear Lake and Soda Springs there is a great need for visitors to know the best sites to visit and how to get to them. This site will provide critical information about weather forecasts, maps, and events that will assist visitors in planning there next trip. The website will also appeal to locals who are planning trips for high school events and other outdoor activities.
