Target Audience

The target audience will include people from the intermountain west including eastern Oregon, western Wyoming, southern Idaho and central Utah. Typical visitors will be families who are planning boating trips to Bear Lake. Also included will be visiting anglers and hunters looking for prime fishing and hunting areas.

Personas

  • Persona 1: Dallin owns a small construction business in Riverton, Utah. His wife and 4 kids always look forward to their monthly boating trips to Bear Lake in the summer months. They love to explore and to find fun places to eat nearby. Dallin typically checks the weather forecast on his phone to decide if they should make the trip. His kids love to be in the water and his wife loves to spend time relaxing with the family. Dallin enjoys using the site because it gives him all the information he needs to plan his trips on one page.
  • Persona 2: McKenna has been fly fishing since she could pick up a rod. She is a Biology major at Utah State in Logan, Utah. Born and raised in Pocatello, she frequently makes weekend trips to Idaho to go fishing and visit her family. Her student apartment doesn't have much room for her to tie flies so she often picks up new flies at local bait shops. She scans the website to learn about fishing conditions and to study maps for new fishing spots.

Scenarios

  1. Dallin checks the website for boating conditions and water levels.
  2. Dallin searches for local hotels and inns in which his family could stay.
  3. Mckenna checks the weather forecast on the site to plan where she is going to fish.
  4. Mckenna's boyfriend wants to go hiking on a Saturday morning. She searches the website for local trails.
  5. Dallin recently bought a mountain bike and looks for popular trails to ride on.
