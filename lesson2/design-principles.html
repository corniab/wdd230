Repetition

Etsy

etsy.com

Etsy uses repetition with circular images. They also use a 2x3 grid that draws the user's eye.

Visual Hierarchy

Evernote

evernote.com

Evernote uses a bold header to grab your attention. Your eyes is then drawn to the green call to action and then to the green logo at the top.

Clean Design

TypeForm

typeform.com

Typeform uses a clean layout to really highlight their call to action. Simple design that is executed well.
