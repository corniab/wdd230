Dynamite

Famous for its history as the filming location of the comedy Napolean Dynamite, Preston is a small farming community in Southern Idaho. Preston was settled by pioneers in the 19th century. Visitors to Preston can enjoy the nearby Glendale and Oneida Narrows reservoirs. There are also many hiking trails close by.
