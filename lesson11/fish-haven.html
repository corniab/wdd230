Fun in Fish Haven!

Nestled on the shore of Bear Lake, Fish Haven offers more than what is immediately apparent. First settled in 1864, Fish Haven was named for its enjoyable fishing and sport. Today, many people enjoy boating and fishing on Bear Lake while relaxing at the many local resorts. In addition, you won't be disappointed by the local eateries. Fish Haven is the perfect getaway!
