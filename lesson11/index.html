Welcome

Southern Idaho boasts some of the most beautiful country in the world. This small region of the Mountain West is one of nature's greatest getaways. Anglers and sportsman alike travel from all over to enjoy this small slice of paradise. With a good mix of all four seasons, visitors and locals enjoy all kinds of outdoor activities. In addition, there are numerous local events with a statewide draw happening year round. We at Southern Idaho Weather are dedicated to bringing you the most up to date information about weather and events. Don't miss out on your next adventure!
