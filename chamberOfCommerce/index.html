Our Mission

Innovation

As a Chamber we are committed to innovation. We have worked tirelessly with city and state government to bring cutting edge companies to our area. From technology to manufacturing, the Chamber continues to be a leader in the Salt Lake Valley.

Integrity

We are committed to building trust in South Jordan. With our local partners we have established ethical business practices which strengthen and protect our community. Members of the Chamber receive professional guidance in creating and practicing good business.

Connection

We are a tight-knit community of businesses from small to big. The Chamber is a powerful tool for networking and expanding the market for businesses. The Chamber is a meeting ground for small business owners, executives, and entrepreneurs.
